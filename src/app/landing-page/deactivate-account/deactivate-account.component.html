How To Deactivate Tobopay Account

Below are quick steps on how to deactivate and delete your Tobopay account.

1. First of all, locate your profile page at the button right corner, then navigate to settings.

2. Then go to deactivate account. Click on Deactivate Account to continue. Then confirm account deactivation.

Note: that by deactivating your account, you lose all your Tobopay data and information.
